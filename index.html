Chennai Super Kings (CSK) :

Led by the iconic MS Dhoni , CSK is a powerhouse known for its consistent performance . Their home ground, the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai , often erupts in a sea of yellow , their signature color. CSK holds the record for most IPL trophies with five , with their most recent win coming in 2023 .
